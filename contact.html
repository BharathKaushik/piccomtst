Our Wedding Photography Process


We believe that wedding photography should be effortless and stress-free for you. Here’s how it works:

📅 1. Inquiry & Consultation
Send us a message with your wedding date, location, and any details you’d like to share. We’ll set up a consultation to learn more about your vision.

✍️ 2. Booking & Planning
Once you decide to book, we’ll secure your date with a signed contract and a deposit. We’ll also help you with timeline planning to ensure a smooth photography experience.

📸 3. Your Wedding Day
Relax and enjoy every moment while we capture all the emotions, details, and magic of your big day.

🖼️ 4. Editing & Delivery
We carefully edit your photos, ensuring they are timeless and beautiful. Your final gallery will be delivered within 6 weeks, ready for you to cherish forever.
